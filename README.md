# Pizza Sales Analysis Project by Python (EDA)

This project focuses on exploring and analyzing pizza sales performance using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) techniques. The main objective is to extract business insights from sales data to help pizza store management understand sales patterns, customer behavior, and product performance.

üìå Project Objective

Understand sales trends, order patterns, and best-selling pizza categories.

Identify which pizza sizes, types, and categories contribute most to revenue.

Provide actionable business insights to maximize sales and optimize marketing strategy.

üìÇ Dataset Source

Dataset comes from YouTube tutorial: "Pizza Sales Analysis" by Data Tutorial

Data contains information related to pizza orders such as:
- Order ID
- Pizza Type, Size, Category
- Quantity, Price, Revenue
- Order Time

üìä Business Questions Answered

- What is the total revenue generated?
- How many pizzas were sold in total?
- Which category and size of pizzas perform best?
- Which pizzas are the top and bottom performers?
- What is the average order value and average pizzas per order?
- What are the sales trends by day, month, and time of day?

üß† Business Insights
- The pizza business generated $817,860.05 in total revenue.
- A total of 49,574 pizzas were sold across 21,350 customer orders.
- Best Performing Category:
Veggie pizzas generated the highest revenue, followed by Chicken and Supreme.
Best Performing Size:
Large (L) pizzas are the clear top revenue contributor, indicating customers prefer larger pizzas over smaller sizes.
- Top 5 pizzas by revenue:
1. The Thai Chicken Pizza
2. The Barbecue Chicken Pizza
3. The California Chicken Pizza
4. The Classic Deluxe Pizza
5. The Spicy Italian Pizza

- Bottom 5 pizzas by revenue:
1. The Brie Carre Pizza
2. The Green Garden Pizza
3. The Spinach Supreme Pizza
4. The Mediterranean Pizza
5. The Spinach Pesto Pizza

- Average Order Value: $38.31
  
Average Pizzas per Order: 2.32 pizzas per transaction

- Highest Order Day: Friday

Highest Sales Month: July

Highest Ordering Time: 12:00 ‚Äì 13:00 (Lunch Time)
